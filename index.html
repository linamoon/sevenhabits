About

The Seven Habits of Highly Effective People, first published in 1989, is a business and self-help book written by Stephen R. Covey.

It has been a top-seller for last 26 years due to the simple reason that it ignores trends and pop psychology for proven principles of fairness, integrity, honesty, and human dignity.

Independence

  1. Be proactive.
  2. Begin with the end in mind.
  3. Be productive.

Interdependence

  1. Think win-win.
  2. Seek first to understand, then to be understood.
  3. Synergize.

Continous Improvements

  1. Sharpen the saw.

Take time out from production to build production capacity through personal renewal of the physical, mental, social/emotional, and spiritual dimensions. Maintain a balance among these dimensions.
